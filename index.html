College of Engineering Vadakara

The College of Engineering Vadakara (CEV), established in 1999, is a premier institution under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE), Government of Kerala. Located in the scenic Kozhikode district, CEV offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology, including B.Tech in Civil, Computer Science, Electronics & Communication, Electrical & Electronics, and Information Technology, as well as MCA. Known for its academic excellence and serene environment, the college fosters innovation and skill development among students, preparing them for a dynamic future.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, CEV continues to contribute significantly to the engineering community, emphasizing practical learning and research. The campus, nestled in Mandarathore, Vatakara, provides an ideal setting for holistic education and personal growth.
